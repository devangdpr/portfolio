# 📸 How to Add Your Photo

## Quick Steps:

### 1. Prepare Your Photo
- Use a square photo (recommended: 500x500px or larger)
- Best formats: JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- Make sure it's a clear headshot or portrait

### 2. Add Your Photo to the Project
- Save your photo in the same folder as `index.html`
- Rename it to: `your-photo.jpg` (or keep the name and update the code)

### 3. Update the HTML (if you use a different filename)
Open `index.html` and find line 68:
```html
<img src="your-photo.jpg" alt="Your Photo" class="profile-photo">
```

Change `your-photo.jpg` to your actual filename:
```html
<img src="my-picture.jpg" alt="Your Photo" class="profile-photo">
```

## 🎨 Photo Effects Included:

✅ **3D Floating Animation** - Your photo gently floats up and down
✅ **Rotating Border** - Animated border that rotates around your photo
✅ **Glow Effect** - Pulsing glow behind the photo
✅ **Hover Effect** - Photo scales up when you hover over it
✅ **Orbiting Circles** - Decorative circles that orbit around
✅ **Smooth Shadows** - Professional depth effect

## 💡 Tips:

- **Square photos work best** - They fit perfectly in the circular frame
- **Good lighting** - Make sure your face is well-lit
- **Simple background** - Solid colors or blurred backgrounds look professional
- **High quality** - Use at least 500x500px for crisp display

## 🖼️ Don't Have a Photo Yet?

You can use a placeholder from these free sites:
- https://unsplash.com (search "portrait")
- https://pexels.com (search "headshot")
- Or use an avatar generator like https://avatar.iran.liara.run/public
